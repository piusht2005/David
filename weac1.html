Choosing 8–9 Subjects for WAEC

In WAEC, students are expected to register for 8–9 subjects. Choosing the right mix is essential for career alignment and success in tertiary education.

Key Subject Areas:

  • Compulsory: English Language, Mathematics
  • Science Students: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Agricultural Science
  • Commercial Students: Economics, Commerce, Accounting
  • Art Students: Literature, CRS/IRS, Government, Yoruba/Igbo/Hausa
  • Vocational: Technical Drawing, Food & Nutrition, ICT

Discuss with your teacher or guidance counselor to align subjects with your future career path.
