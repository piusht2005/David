NECO Result Checking & Verification

After your NECO exams, it’s important to know how to properly check and verify your result. Here’s a step-by-step guide:

Steps to Check NECO Results:

  1. Buy a NECO result checking token (can be purchased online)
  2. Visit results.neco.gov.ng
  3. Enter your exam year, registration number, and token
  4. Click "Check Result" to view it instantly

Print and save a copy of your result for future use (admissions, scholarships, NYSC, etc.).
