Malpractice Prevention Guidelines

Exam malpractice can ruin your academic career and damage your future. NECO has zero tolerance for cheating, and students must take the guidelines seriously.

Tips to Avoid Malpractice:

  • Don't take phones or unauthorized materials into the exam hall
  • Avoid talking or copying from others during the exam
  • Stay focused and manage your time wisely
  • Report any suspicious activity to supervisors

Remember, it’s better to fail with integrity than pass through cheating and get caught. Work hard, and trust your preparation.
