JAMB Score Calculator & Performance Estimator

JAMB exams are scored out of 400. Here’s how you can calculate and estimate your score before the official results drop:

How to Calculate:

  • Total Questions: Usually 180 questions (4 subjects)
  • Each Question: Carries roughly 2.22 marks
  • Formula: Total correct × 2.22 = Estimated score

Example:

If you answered 140 questions correctly, your estimated score is:
140 × 2.22 = 310.8

Use online tools or simple calculators to get quick estimates. This helps manage your expectations and plan better.
