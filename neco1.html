Comprehensive Subject Coverage in NECO

NECO (National Examinations Council) offers a wide range of subjects across the Arts, Sciences, Commercial, and Technical categories. Every student is expected to register for at least 8–9 subjects.

General Subject Structure:

  • English Language (compulsory)
  • Mathematics (compulsory)
  • Three to four subjects based on student’s stream (Science, Commercial, or Art)
  • At least one Nigerian language (Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, etc.)
  • Vocational or elective subjects (Agricultural Science, Technical Drawing, Home Economics, etc.)

Make sure to choose subjects relevant to your future career goals and university requirements.
