Practical Exams & Virtual Lab Resources

WAEC Science practicals (Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Agric) are a major component of the exam. They test your ability to apply theory to real-life lab settings.

How to Prepare:

  • Study past practical questions and mark schemes
  • Use online simulations (like Labster, PhET) to practice experiments
  • Join school-organized lab sessions before the exam
  • Memorize key apparatus names and functions

Even if your school lacks good labs, virtual simulations can help you visualize and understand how practicals work.
