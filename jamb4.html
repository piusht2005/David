180 Questions in 2 Hours – Simulation Practice

The JAMB exam requires students to answer 180 questions in just 120 minutes. That gives you less than 1 minute per question. Simulating this timing is key.

How to Simulate:

  • Set a timer for 2 hours
  • Use past questions or CBT apps
  • Practice switching between subjects
  • Mark questions you’re unsure about and review them at the end

Why It Works:

It improves time management, accuracy, and speed, giving you a real sense of exam pressure.
