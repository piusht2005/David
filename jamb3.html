CBT Format Mastery: Tips & Techniques

JAMB is fully Computer-Based Test (CBT), and many students struggle not with the questions but with the system. Here's how to master it:

Top Tips:

  • Practice with official JAMB CBT apps
  • Understand how to use a mouse or trackpad confidently
  • Use the “flag” or “review” feature for tough questions
  • Don't waste time on one question — move and return later
  • Use keyboard shortcuts if available (some centers allow this)

With regular CBT practice, your speed and confidence will increase massively.
