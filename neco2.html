NECO Registration Deadlines & Updates

NECO registration is time-sensitive and must be completed through your school or at approved centers. Missing the deadline could mean missing the exam entirely.

Typical Registration Timeline:

  • Start: February – April (varies yearly)
  • Deadline: Usually by late May or early June
  • Late Registration: Allowed with a penalty fee

Stay updated by following official NECO news or asking your school administrator.
