Preparing for WAEC's CBT Transition (2026)

WAEC has announced plans to transition to Computer-Based Testing (CBT) starting in 2026. This means students need to start preparing now for a digital exam experience.

What You Can Do Now:

  • Practice past questions on CBT apps and websites
  • Familiarize yourself with typing and using a mouse
  • Attend schools or centers that offer CBT mock exams
  • Stay updated with WAEC’s announcements on the transition

This change will benefit digital-savvy students, so take the lead and start practicing now.
