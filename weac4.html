WAEC Result Verification with Scratch Cards

After the exam, the next important thing is checking and verifying your result. Here’s how to do it safely and correctly:

How to Check Your Result:

  1. Purchase a WAEC result scratch card (from vendors or online)
  2. Visit waecdirect.org
  3. Enter your exam year, number, and PIN on the scratch card
  4. Click "Submit" to view your result instantly

Keep a printed copy and a screenshot of your result for university applications.
