JAMB Registration Guide & Deadlines

To avoid last-minute rush and mistakes, it’s important to understand the JAMB registration process fully.

Steps to Register:

  1. Create a JAMB profile via the official website
  2. Generate your profile code via SMS
  3. Purchase JAMB ePIN from banks or online
  4. Complete registration at a certified CBT center
  5. Get your exam slip with date and center

Deadline:

Registration usually runs from January to February. Always follow JAMB’s official social media pages and website for updates.
