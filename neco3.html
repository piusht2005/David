NECO Practical Examination Preparation

For science students, practical exams are key components of the final grade. Subjects like Chemistry, Biology, Physics, and Agric Science include hands-on sessions.

Preparation Tips:

  • Review diagrams and common lab tools
  • Study past practical questions and answers
  • Attend school lab sessions regularly
  • Practice drawing and labeling neat diagrams

Don’t just cram — understand how to perform the experiments and how to observe accurately.
