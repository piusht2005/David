Core Subjects: English & Mathematics

English Language and Mathematics are the foundation of every WAEC result. They are compulsory and often determine your overall success.

Why They're Crucial:

  • They are required for admission into any course in Nigerian universities
  • Failure in any of them can invalidate your WAEC result (i.e., not qualified for most courses)
  • They're used to calculate your O’Level points in admissions like JAMB and Post-UTME

Put extra effort into mastering these two subjects. Use past questions, attend tutorials, and study smart.
