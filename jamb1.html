Subject Combination Guide Based on Desired Courses

Choosing the right subject combination for JAMB is one of the most important steps when applying to Nigerian universities. A wrong subject combo can disqualify you even with a high score.

Popular Courses and Their Required Subjects:

  • Medicine & Surgery: English, Biology, Chemistry, Physics
  • Engineering (Mechanical, Electrical, Civil): English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry
  • Law: English, Literature in English, Government, CRS/IRS
  • Mass Communication: English, Literature in English, Government, CRS/IRS
  • Computer Science: English, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry or Biology
  • Business Administration: English, Mathematics, Economics, Commerce

Always check the JAMB brochure or official school websites for updated subject requirements.
